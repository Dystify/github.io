Unsupported Devices

The following devices, which were supported by some past hardware, will not be supported by the NX.

  • Autostereoscopic LCD screen
  • Microphone (headphone jack is provided)
  • RGB camera
  • Infrared communication feature

The stylus and microSD card is not included.
