About @NintendoNewsBot:

This bot collects Nintendo related articles from the best English news websites around the world and automatically posts them to Twitter as soon as they're published.
It aims to reach top accuracy (only post content that really is about Nintendo) and fast speed (post as soon as the news hit the web).

Sites currently supported:
YouTube.com/user/Nintendo
MyNintendoNews.com/
Nintendoeverything.com/category/news/
Eurogamer.net/?type=news
WSJ.com/news/technology
IGN.com/articles?tags=news

Keywords the bot is looking for on sites that are not purely Nintendo:
Nintendo,NX,Pokemon,Zelda,Smash Bros,3DS,Kirby,Mario,Splatoon,Pikmin,Wii U,WiiU

It's currently only looking for very few keywords, as it's still being tested. A lot more keywords will be added soon.
