2.5 POWER Menu

The menu that appears when you hold down the POWER Button for at least three seconds. Choose to either turn the NX off or enter sleep mode.

Features

The POWER Menu has the following characteristics.

  • It can be invoked at any time.
  • Appears full-screen.
  • An application will not pause. It will continue to run in the background while the POWER Menu is displayed. In that situation, application input will be received by the POWER Menu.
  • You will automatically enter sleep mode if a state of inactivity continues for at least ten seconds.

POWER Menu Options

The POWER Menu options are as follows.

During TV Use (Console Mode) indicates whether the item can be configured during TV mode, and During LCD Screen Use (Handheld Mode) indicates whether it can be configured during handheld mode.

Table 2-2 POWER Menu Options
Item Description During TV Use (Console Mode) During LCD Screen Use (Handheld Mode)
Sleep

Puts the NX to sleep.

Note: You can enter sleep mode by just tapping the POWER Button.

 ✔ ✔
Reboot Restarts the NX. ✔ ✔
Power OFF Turns the NX off. ✔ ✔
Back

Closes the POWER Menu, returning to the previous screen.

The following actions may also be possible.

  • Pressing the B Button
  • Pressing the HOME Button
